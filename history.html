Atlantis Bahamas: History

Atlantis opened on December, 1994. It was created by Sol Kerzner after he purchased the resort on May, 1994.
It started as a 1,147 room resort, with 14 acres for swimming pools, waterfalls, a lazy river ride, and six exhibit lagoons including a clear underwater tunnel in a shark habitat.
